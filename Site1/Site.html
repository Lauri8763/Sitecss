    Escape From Tarkov

    What is Escape From Tarkov?

    Escape from Tarkov is a hardcore, realistic online multiplayer story game that combines the genres of FPS/TPS, combat simulation and RPG with MMO elements. With each passing day, the situation in the Norwyn region becomes more and more confusing. Unceasing fighting in the streets of Tarkov has caused mass panic among the population, filling the roads leading out of it with refugees. However, there are those who have decided to stay, seeing in this situation an opportunity to make a profit at someone else's expense. Taking new reality as a given, unorganized loners of Tarkov's inhabitants - the Wild Ones - began to form well-armed gangs, fighting with each other for the redistribution of the city. Today all of Tarkov is divided by invisible lines into zones of influence of various factions. Armed bandits will stop at nothing in their thirst for profit and are not afraid of killing civilians or directly confronting two private military companies.

    Pick your side

    Players will have to embed themselves in the role of one of the mercenaries who survived the early stages of the Tarkovski conflict. After choosing one of the sides - USEC or BEAR - the player character begins their arduous journey in search of a way out of the city. The exits from Tarkov are blocked by UN peacekeepers and Russian troops, all supply lines have been cut off, and communication with the commanders has been lost. Under such circumstances, it is up to everyone to choose how to survive, what to do and how to get out of the megalopolis plunged into chaos.
